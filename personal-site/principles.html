Principles

Core beliefs and values that guide my approach to AI research, development, and collaboration.

Research Excellence Through Rigor

Quality research requires systematic evaluation, reproducible results, and honest reporting of limitations. I believe in building robust benchmarks and sharing both successes and failures.

Read more→

Open Collaboration Over Competition

The biggest challenges in AI require collective intelligence. I prioritize sharing knowledge, contributing to open source, and building on others' work rather than competing in isolation.

Read more→

Building for Real-World Impact

Research should ultimately serve human needs. I focus on creating tools and systems that work reliably in practice, not just in controlled lab environments.

Read more→

Embrace Continuous Learning

The field moves quickly, and humility is essential. I stay curious, admit when I'm wrong, and constantly update my understanding based on new evidence.

Read more→

Responsible AI Development

With powerful technology comes responsibility. I consider the broader implications of AI systems, prioritizing safety, fairness, and beneficial outcomes for society.
